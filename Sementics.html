Plants

With over 380,000 known species, plants are essential organisms ranging from microscopic algae to 379-foot tall redwoods, producing most of Earth's oxygen and food

They use photosynthesis to turn sunlight into energy, can communicate through underground chemical signals, respond to sound, and, like strawberries, sometimes bear seeds on the outside
